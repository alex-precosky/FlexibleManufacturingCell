U4
 ProcessUnitController > signalDone
 bounded 4/ min(min_i, crane_min) s
 No more than 4 times in min_i seconds, or crane_max seconds will this be called, where min_i is the minimum processing time a blank can spend in a unit, and crane_min is the minimum time a crane can take to complete a job of moving a blank between two locations.
U2,U4
 CraneController > signalDone
 bounded 2/ crane_min s
 No more than 2 times in crane_min seconds, where crane_min is the minimum amount of time the crane could ever take to move a blank between two locations.

U1
 FeedBelt < turn_on pa: [0.01:10 s] hard
 The time taken to turn on the feed belt from the time of request.  Has a hard minimum bound to prevent the slipping of the belt if it starts too quickly.
U1
 FeedBelt < turn_off
 pe: [0.01:0.05 s] hard
 The feed belt should be turned off before a part falls off.  Has a minimum bound because if the belt is stopped to quickly, the part's momentum might send it into a roll.
